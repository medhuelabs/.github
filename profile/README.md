## Welcome to MedHue Labs LLC

At MedHue Labs we build where science meets art, and we try to take a human-first approach on our products.

Our product, Tmates, lets you create AI teammates, talk to them like coworkers, delegate tasks, and get results while you stay focused. They keep running when you're offline, learn from feedback, and be tailored to life and work.

Anyone can build a teammate with our TmatesAgentsSDK. Feel free to explore our open source resources or reach out.
